How Triple Witching Works

üßô The Three "Witches"
Triple witching occurs when stock index futures, stock index options, and single stock options all expire on the same day ‚Äî the third Friday of March, June, September, and December.
üìà Stock Index Futures

Products: E-mini S&P 500 (ES), E-mini Nasdaq-100 (NQ), E-mini Russell 2000

Settlement: Cash settled at SOQ (Special Opening Quotation)

Timing: AM settlement based on opening prices

üìä Stock Index Options

Products: SPX, NDX, RUT options

Settlement: Cash settled at SOQ

Timing: AM settlement (9:40-10:00 AM ET typically)

üéØ Single Stock Options

Products: Options on individual equities

Settlement: Physical delivery (100 shares per contract)

Timing: PM settlement at market close

Market Maker Mechanics & Delta Hedging
IMPORTANT ‚ñº

Market makers who sell options aim to remain delta-neutral, earning profits from bid-ask spreads rather than directional bets.

Delta Hedging
When customers buy calls, dealers buy the underlying stock. When customers buy puts, dealers short the underlying. This creates constant rebalancing pressure.
Gamma Effect
Near expiration, at-the-money options have extremely high gamma, forcing market makers to rebalance with increasing frequency. SPX options alone carry ~$80 billion of gross gamma.
Pinning Effect
Prices gravitate toward strike prices with high open interest because of gamma hedging dynamics. Academic research shows "max pain" strategies generate 0.4% weekly returns during expiration weeks.
Open Interest Impact
SPX options represent ~20% of total S&P 500 market cap (~$5.2 trillion), making hedging flows among "the most important non-fundamental flows in equity markets."

Settlement Process: AM vs PM
TIMING ‚ñº

AM Settlement (Index Products)

  • üìç Special Opening Quotation (SOQ) ‚Äî calculated from each index component's first reported opening sale
  • üìç Timing: Typically finalized by 9:40-10:00 AM ET
  • üìç Gap Risk: SOQ can deviate -1.0% to +1.0% from actual index open
  • üìç Note: 11 of 33 quarterly settlements (2009-2017) saw SOQ exceed day's actual trading high

PM Settlement (Stock Options)

  • üìç Physical Delivery ‚Äî 100 shares per contract
  • üìç Timing: Market close at 4:00 PM ET
  • üìç MOC Orders: Massive market-on-close order flow
  • üìç Volume: Closing auction ~$80B additional notional

Triple vs Quadruple Witching
‚ñº

"Quadruple witching" was the term used from 2002-2020 when single stock futures traded on OneChicago. That exchange closed in September 2020, so technically we're back to "triple" witching.

However, practitioners sometimes still reference "quad witch" to include index rebalances (S&P, FTSE, Russell, Nasdaq) that coincide with these dates and add approximately 40% of market-on-close order flow.

The rebalancing effect from major indices (especially Russell reconstitution in June) can actually exceed the derivatives expiration impact in terms of price movement.

Trading Strategies

‚≠ê Highest Edge Strategy: Pre-Expiration Rally

Go long Monday open of witching week, exit Thursday close. Average gain: +0.55% per trade, 65% win rate across 128 backtested trades.

üìà Entry Rules
  • ‚úì Enter long S&P 500 exposure (SPY, ES futures, or equivalent) at Monday open of witching week
  • ‚úì Alternative: Enter T-9 (Monday prior week) for full rally capture (+0.82% average)
  • ‚úì Size position at 70-80% of normal to account for elevated volatility
  • ‚úì Set stops at 2.5 ATR (wider than normal 2 ATR)
üìâ Exit Rules
  • ‚úì Exit all positions by Thursday 3:30 PM ET ‚Äî BEFORE Friday
  • ‚úì If Wednesday shows strong gains (+0.5%+), consider taking partial profits
  • ‚úì Use limit orders only ‚Äî never market orders approaching expiration
  • ‚úó NEVER hold into Friday ‚Äî negative expected value
‚ö†Ô∏è High Risk Environment
Triple witching Friday has only a 14% win rate since 2021. The recommended strategy is do not trade, or trade with extreme caution and reduced size.
üéØ If You Must Trade
  • ‚úì Trade only 10:30 AM - 2:00 PM window (midday lull)
  • ‚úì Reduce position size to 30-50% of normal
  • ‚úì Use defined-risk strategies only (spreads, not naked positions)
  • ‚úì Monitor max pain levels ‚Äî prices gravitate toward them
  • ‚úì Close ALL positions by 2:30 PM at the latest
üö´ What to Avoid
  • ‚úó NEVER trade the opening 30 minutes (SOQ settlement chaos)
  • ‚úó NEVER trade the final hour (3-4 PM "witching hour")
  • ‚úó NEVER use market orders ‚Äî slippage is severe
  • ‚úó NEVER hold expiring options through close
  • ‚úó NEVER chase moves ‚Äî reversals are violent

üìâ Post-Witching Weakness Pattern
The week following triple witching has been negative in 14 of 21 years (66%). September's following week is historically the worst ‚Äî "rarely positive over 30 years and averages greater than 1% declines."
üîÑ Reversal Setup Criteria

Watch for these conditions Monday-Tuesday after witching:

  • ‚úì VIX spike of 15%+ from pre-witching levels
  • ‚úì S&P declines 1%+ from Thursday close
  • ‚úì Volume returns to normal levels (hedging complete)
  • ‚úì Put/call ratio normalizes from elevated levels

üí∞ Premium Selling Strategy

Timing: Establish positions 1-2 weeks BEFORE witching when IV is elevated.

  • ‚úì Sell premium 7-14 days out from expiration
  • ‚úì Close positions by Wednesday of witching week
  • ‚úì Use defined-risk spreads (iron condors, verticals)
  • ‚úó DO NOT sell premium directly into expiration day
üìÖ Calendar Spread Strategy

Setup: Buy longer-dated options, sell expiring options.

  • ‚úì Benefits from accelerated time decay on short leg
  • ‚úì Enter 5-7 days before expiration
  • ‚úì Target strikes near max pain level
  • ‚úì Close when short leg reaches 80% profit or Thursday EOD
